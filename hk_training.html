🇭🇰 What We Offer

🏢 The Product — Private Limited Company (Ltd)

Standard HK legal entity. Globally recognised. One director + one shareholder — can be the same person. No minimum capital.

Registry is public → Nominee solution

We place a nominee director + nominee shareholder (HK residents). Two documents protect the real owner: Trust Declaration + General Power of Attorney. Nominee has zero real influence.

Company Secretary — mandatory by law

Every HK company must have a local secretary. We provide it. Handles filings only — no access to operations or accounts.

No licence needed for trading

Construction materials, supply chain, logistics — no extra licence. Only financial services (banking, crypto, insurance) require one.

💰 Taxation — Territorial System

  • 0% on all international income — goods flowing China → Europe/Saudi Arabia are not taxed
  • 0% on dividends for non-residents
  • 0% VAT — does not exist in HK
  • 8.25% only on income sourced inside HK (first HKD 2M)
Key rule: goods and money must NOT physically pass through Hong Kong. The company is the legal intermediary on paper — contracts and invoices flow through HK, goods ship directly.

"Do you have any existing company structure, or are you starting from scratch?"
→ If he already has a company somewhere, we need to understand the full picture. Maybe HK works as an addition, not a replacement.
"Is privacy important to you — do you want your name to appear publicly in the registry?"

"Is there any deadline you're working towards — a supplier contract, a buyer payment, a launch date?"
→ Urgency changes everything. If he has a deadline, we can use that to drive momentum. Registration is 1–2 weeks — that's fast and worth highlighting if timing matters.
