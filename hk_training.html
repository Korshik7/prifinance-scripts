🇭🇰 What We Offer — Hong Kong

🏢 Company Structure — Private Limited Company (Ltd)

The standard Hong Kong legal entity — globally recognised, simple to set up, no minimum share capital required. One director and one shareholder is enough. Both can be the same person.

Registry

Hong Kong's company registry is public — director and shareholder names are visible to anyone. Solution: nominee director + nominee shareholder (both HK residents). Two legal documents protect the real owner:

  • Trust Declaration — confirms you are the true beneficial owner
  • General Power of Attorney — gives you full control. Nominee has zero real influence.

Mandatory Company Secretary

Required by law — every HK company must have a local secretary. We provide this. They handle statutory filings only — no access to company operations or accounts.

No license needed for trading

Standard trading, logistics, supply chain — no additional licence required. Licences are only needed for financial services (banking, insurance, crypto funds).

💰 Taxation — Territorial System

This is the core advantage. Hong Kong only taxes income sourced within Hong Kong. If your business operates internationally — buying in China, selling to Europe or Saudi Arabia — that profit is not taxed in HK at all.

  • 0% — on profit from international operations (offshore income)
  • 0% — on dividends paid to non-residents
  • 0% — VAT does not exist in Hong Kong
  • 8.25% — only if income is sourced inside HK (first HKD 2M)
  • 16.5% — on HK-sourced income above HKD 2M
Key rule: goods and money must NOT physically pass through Hong Kong territory. The company acts as a legal intermediary on paper only — contracts and invoices flow through HK, goods go directly China → Europe/Saudi Arabia.
