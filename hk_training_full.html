🇭🇰 What We Offer — Hong Kong

🏢 Company Structure — Private Limited Company (Ltd)

Standard HK legal entity. Globally recognised. One director + one shareholder — can be the same person. No minimum share capital.

Registry is public → Nominee solution

We place a nominee director + nominee shareholder (HK residents). Two documents protect the real owner: Trust Declaration + General Power of Attorney. Nominee has zero real influence.

Company Secretary — mandatory by law

Every HK company must have a local secretary. We provide it. Handles filings only — no access to operations or accounts.

No licence needed for trading

Construction materials, logistics, supply chain — no extra licence. Only financial services (banking, crypto, insurance) require one.

💰 Taxation — Territorial System

  • 0% on all international income — goods flowing China → Europe/Saudi are not taxed
  • 0% on dividends for non-residents
  • 0% VAT — does not exist in HK
  • 8.25% only on income sourced inside HK (first HKD 2M)
  • 16.5% on HK-sourced income above HKD 2M
Key rule: goods and money must NOT physically pass through Hong Kong. Company is the legal intermediary on paper — contracts flow through HK, goods ship directly.
